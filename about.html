About Us

Every great meal has a story, and Nasi Anje's legend began in Inanam Sentral with a focus on Fried Chicken Curry Rice. Recognizing a market gap, the stall combined crispy fried chicken with a unique sauce, leading to rapid growth and accessibility in locations like Imago Shopping Mall. The dish is distinguished by three core elements: fragrant rice infused with lemongrass, thick and flavorful signature gravy, and perfectly crispy chicken. This combination fosters customer loyalty, as Nasi Anje delivers high-quality, unforgettable meals at an affordable price, proving that legendary flavor should be accessible.
